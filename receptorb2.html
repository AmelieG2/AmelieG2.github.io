Receptor ⓶

Localisation

This receptor is a receptor coupled to a Gs protein, found once again in smooth muscles such as blood vessels, respiratory airways and gastro-intestinal tract. It is a postsynaptic receptor, involved into relaxation of the smooth muscles.

Agonists

The agonists for that receptor are catecholamines.

Pathway

In turns, the Gs protein activates an enzyme called Adenylate Cyclase (AC)
The AC is an enzyme responsible for the synthesis of the cyclic adenylate monophosphate (cAMP). The cAMP concentration in the cell will then rise.
