Receptor α 1

Localisation

This receptor is a receptor coupled to a Gq protein, found mostly in smooth muscles such as blood vessels, respiratory airways and gastro-intestinal tract. It is a mostly postsynaptic receptor, which activation will lead to a contraction of the smooth muscles.

Agonists

Antagonists

Phentanolamine.

Pathway

When an agonist binds to the α1 receptor, it activates the Gq protein
In turns, the Gq protein activates an enzyme called the Phospholipase Cβ (PLCβ)

IP3 acts on a receptor at the surface of the endoplasmic reticulum
